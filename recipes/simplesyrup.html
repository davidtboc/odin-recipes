Simple Syrup

Home

Description: This is an easy recipe for simple syrup because it has two ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup water

Directions

    Step 1
  1. Gather all ingredients.
    2. Step 2
  2. Combine sugar and water in a medium saucepan over medium heat.
    3. Step 3
  3. Bring to a boil, stirring, until sugar has dissolved. Allow to cool.
    4. Step 4
  4. Pour into a clean jar and seal with a tight-fitting lid; store in the refrigerator for up to one month.
